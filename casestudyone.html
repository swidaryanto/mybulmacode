Name Card Page Redesign

Product Design UI / UX

A redesign of Tjetak Name Card page to improve the customer experience when customer want to order.

The Problem

A customer wants to print business cards online through the website: Tjetak.com. This customer does not have any preferences related to the business card they want to print, but they want to:
  1. Make a business card with a cheap price and,
  2. Make a bussiness card then completed it in quick time (assume in 1 day only).

A Redesign is needed because the current interface of name card page is only based on requirement which is most of people doesn't know much about it.

Current Tjetak Name Card Page

My Role

I am responsible for all aspects of the UX process, from creating the personas, user flow, user journey, lo-fi until it completed by hi-fi design.

A Persona

The personas is about a new startup founder names Alexander want to create a new name card for his business with fast and cheap.

Result - Part I

Based on the background of the user journey above and background what is the problem that user faced. The result of page that I created is:
  1. I make the estimated time for completed order at the first place.
  2. For the price it's flexible, so the user could input by themself from the lowest price and to higher price, and it would impact to card name category.
  3. The last is I put the delivery option and type of delivery.
